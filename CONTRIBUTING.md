## Governance and Copyright Ownership

"Valdock Authors" refers to the collective group of individuals who have
contributed code or documentation to the Valdock project under the terms
of the Elastic License 2.0.

The original author and current maintainer of the Valdock project retains
the right to:

- Approve and merge contributions,
- Manage project releases,
- Decide on future licensing or commercial use.

By submitting a contribution, you agree that your work will be distributed
under the Elastic License 2.0 and that the Valdock maintainers may include
it in future versions under the same or an updated license.
